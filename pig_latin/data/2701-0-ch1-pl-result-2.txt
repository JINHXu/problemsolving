APTERCHAY 1ay. Oomingslay. 

Allcay emay Ishmaelay. Omesay earsyay ago—neveray indmay owhay onglay ecisely—havingpray ittlelay oray onay oneymay inay myay ursepay, anday othingnay articularpay otay interestay emay onay oreshay, IAY oughtthay IAY ouldway ailsay aboutay aay ittlelay anday eesay ethay ateryway artpay ofay ethay orldway. Itay isay aay ayway IAY avehay ofay ivingdray offay ethay eensplay anday egulatingray ethay irculationcay. Eneverwhay IAY indfay elfmysay owinggray imgray aboutay ethay outhmay; eneverwhay itay isay aay ampday, izzlydray Ovembernay inay myay oulsay; eneverwhay IAY indfay elfmysay involuntarilyay ausingpay eforebay offincay arehousesway, anday ingingbray upay ethay earray ofay everyay uneralfay IAY eetmay; anday especiallyay eneverwhay myay oshypay etgay uchsay anay upperay andhay ofay emay, atthay itay equiresray aay ongstray oralmay inciplepray otay eventpray emay omfray eliberatelyday eppingstay intoay ethay eetstray, anday ethodicallymay ockingknay eople’spay atshay off—thenay, IAY accountay itay ighhay imetay otay etgay otay easay asay oonsay asay IAY ancay. Isthay isay myay ubstitutesay orfay istolpay anday allbay. Ithway aay ilosophicalphay ourishflay Atocay owsthray imselfhay uponay ishay ordsway; IAY uietlyqay aketay otay ethay ipshay. Erethay isay othingnay urprisingsay inay isthay. Ifay eythay utbay ewknay itay, almostay allay enmay inay eirthay egreeday, omesay imetay oray otheray, erishchay eryvay earlynay ethay amesay eelingsfay owardstay ethay oceanay ithway emay. 

Erethay ownay isay ouryay insularay itycay ofay ethay Anhattoesmay, eltedbay oundray byay arveswhay asay Indianay islesay byay oralcay eefs—commerceray urroundssay itay ithway erhay urfsay. Ightray anday eftlay, ethay eetsstray aketay ouyay aterwardway. Itsay extremeay owntownday isay ethay atterybay, erewhay atthay oblenay olemay isay ashedway byay avesway, anday ooledcay byay eezesbray, ichwhay aay ewfay ourshay eviouspray ereway outay ofay ightsay ofay andlay. Ooklay atay ethay owdscray ofay ater-gazersway erethay. 

Ircumambulatecay ethay itycay ofay aay eamydray Abbathsay afternoonay. Ogay omfray Orlearscay Ookhay otay Oentiescay Ipslay, anday omfray encethay, byay Itehallwhay, orthwardnay. Atwhay oday ouyay ee?—Postedsay ikelay ilentsay entinelssay allay arounday ethay owntay, andstay ousandsthay uponay ousandsthay ofay ortalmay enmay ixedfay inay oceanay everiesray. Omesay eaninglay againstay ethay ilesspay; omesay eatedsay uponay ethay ier-headspay; omesay ookinglay overay ethay ulwarksbay ofay ipsshay omfray Inachay; omesay ighhay aloftay inay ethay iggingray, asay ifay ivingstray otay etgay aay illstay etterbay eawardsay eeppay. Utbay esethay areay allay andsmenlay; ofay eekway aysday entpay upay inay athlay anday aster—tiedplay otay ounterscay, ailednay otay enchesbay, inchedclay otay esksday. Owhay enthay isay isthay? Areay ethay eengray ieldsfay onegay? Atwhay oday eythay erehay? 

Utbay ooklay! erehay omecay oremay owdscray, acingpay aightstray orfay ethay aterway, anday eeminglysay oundbay orfay aay iveday. Angestray! Othingnay illway ontentcay emthay utbay ethay extremestay imitlay ofay ethay andlay; oiteringlay underay ethay adyshay eelay ofay onderyay arehousesway illway otnay ufficesay. Onay. Eythay ustmay etgay ustjay asay ighnay ethay aterway asay eythay ossiblypay ancay ithoutway allingfay inay. Anday erethay eythay and—milesstay ofay em—leaguesthay. Inlandersay allay, eythay omecay omfray aneslay anday alleysay, eetsstray anday avenues—northay, eastay, outhsay, anday estway. Etyay erehay eythay allay uniteay. Elltay emay, oesday ethay agneticmay irtuevay ofay ethay eedlesnay ofay ethay ompassescay ofay allay osethay ipsshay attractay emthay itherthay? 

Onceay oremay. Aysay ouyay areay inay ethay ountrycay; inay omesay ighhay andlay ofay akeslay. Aketay almostay anyay athpay ouyay easeplay, anday entay otay oneay itay arriescay ouyay ownday inay aay aleday, anday eaveslay ouyay erethay byay aay oolpay inay ethay eamstray. Erethay isay agicmay inay itay. Etlay ethay ostmay absent-mindeday ofay enmay ebay ungedplay inay ishay eepestday everies—standray atthay anmay onay ishay egslay, etsay ishay eetfay a-goingay, anday ehay illway infalliblyay eadlay ouyay otay aterway, ifay aterway erethay ebay inay allay atthay egionray. Ouldshay ouyay everay ebay athirstay inay ethay eatgray Americanay esertday, tryay isthay experimentay, ifay ouryay aravancay appenhay otay ebay uppliedsay ithway aay etaphysicalmay ofessorpray. Esyay, asay everyay oneay owsknay, editationmay anday aterway areay eddedway orfay everay. 

Utbay erehay isay anay artistay. Ehay esiresday otay aintpay ouyay ethay eamiestdray, adiestshay, uietestqay, ostmay enchantingay itbay ofay omanticray andscapelay inay allay ethay alleyvay ofay ethay Acosay. Atwhay isay ethay iefchay elementay ehay employsay? Erethay andstay ishay eestray, eachay ithway aay ollowhay unktray, asay ifay aay ermithay anday aay ucifixcray ereway ithinway; anday erehay eepsslay ishay eadowmay, anday erethay eepslay ishay attlecay; anday upay omfray onderyay ottagecay oesgay aay eepyslay okesmay. Eepday intoay istantday oodlandsway indsway aay azymay ayway, eachingray otay overlappingay ursspay ofay ountainsmay athedbay inay eirthay ill-sidehay ueblay. Utbay oughthay ethay icturepay ieslay usthay ancedtray, anday oughthay isthay ine-treepay akesshay ownday itsay ighssay ikelay eaveslay uponay isthay epherd’sshay eadhay, etyay allay ereway ainvay, unlessay ethay epherd’sshay eyeay ereway ixedfay uponay ethay agicmay eamstray eforebay imhay. Ogay isitvay ethay Airiespray inay Unejay, enwhay orfay oresscay onay oresscay ofay ilesmay ouyay adeway ee-deepknay amongay Iger-lilies—whattay isay ethay oneay armchay anting?—Water—thereway isay otnay aay opdray ofay aterway erethay! Ereway Iagaranay utbay aay ataractcay ofay andsay, ouldway ouyay aveltray ouryay ousandthay ilesmay otay eesay itay? Whyay idday ethay oorpay oetpay ofay Ennesseetay, uponay uddenlysay eceivingray otway andfulshay ofay ilversay, eliberateday etherwhay otay uybay imhay aay oatcay, ichwhay ehay adlysay eedednay, oray investay ishay oneymay inay aay edestrianpay iptray otay Ockawayray Eachbay? Whyay isay almostay everyay obustray ealthyhay oybay ithway aay obustray ealthyhay oulsay inay imhay, atay omesay imetay oray otheray azycray otay ogay otay easay? Whyay uponay ouryay irstfay oyagevay asay aay assengerpay, idday ouyay ourselfyay eelfay uchsay aay icalmystay ibrationvay, enwhay irstfay oldtay atthay ouyay anday ouryay ipshay ereway ownay outay ofay ightsay ofay andlay? Whyay idday ethay olday Ersianspay oldhay ethay easay olyhay? Whyay idday ethay Eeksgray ivegay itay aay eparatesay eityday, anday ownay otherbray ofay Ovejay? Urelysay allay isthay isay otnay ithoutway eaningmay. Anday illstay eeperday ethay eaningmay ofay atthay orystay ofay Arcissusnay, owhay ecausebay ehay ouldcay otnay aspgray ethay ormentingtay, ildmay imageay ehay awsay inay ethay ountainfay, ungedplay intoay itay anday asway owneddray. Utbay atthay amesay imageay, eway ourselvesay eesay inay allay iversray anday oceansay. Itay isay ethay imageay ofay ethay ungraspableay antomphay ofay ifelay; anday isthay isay ethay eykay otay itay allay. 

Ownay, enwhay IAY aysay atthay IAY amay inay ethay abithay ofay oinggay otay easay eneverwhay IAY eginbay otay owgray azyhay aboutay ethay eyesay, anday eginbay otay ebay overay onsciouscay ofay myay ungslay, IAY oday otnay eanmay otay avehay itay inferreday atthay IAY everay ogay otay easay asay aay assengerpay. Orfay otay ogay asay aay assengerpay ouyay ustmay eedsnay avehay aay ursepay, anday aay ursepay isay utbay aay agray unlessay ouyay avehay omethingsay inay itay. Esidesbay, assengerspay etgay ea-sick—growsay uarrelsome—don’tqay eepslay ofay ights—donay otnay enjoyay emselvesthay uchmay, asay aay eneralgay ing;—nothay, IAY evernay ogay asay aay assengerpay; ornay, oughthay IAY amay omethingsay ofay aay altsay, oday IAY everay ogay otay easay asay aay Ommodorecay, oray aay Aptaincay, oray aay Ookcay. IAY abandonay ethay oryglay anday istinctionday ofay uchsay officesay otay osethay owhay ikelay emthay. Orfay myay artpay, IAY abominateay allay onorablehay espectableray oilstay, ialstray, anday ibulationstray ofay everyay indkay atsoeverwhay. Itay isay uiteqay asay uchmay asay IAY ancay oday otay aketay arecay ofay elfmysay, ithoutway akingtay arecay ofay ipsshay, arquesbay, igsbray, oonersschay, anday atwhay otnay. Anday asay orfay oinggay asay ook,—thoughcay IAY onfesscay erethay isay onsiderablecay oryglay inay atthay, aay ookcay eingbay aay ortsay ofay officeray onay ip-board—yetshay, omehowsay, IAY evernay anciedfay oilingbray owls;—thoughfay onceay oiledbray, udiciouslyjay utteredbay, anday udgmaticallyjay altedsay anday epperedpay, erethay isay onay oneay owhay illway eakspay oremay espectfullyray, otnay otay aysay everentiallyray, ofay aay oiledbray owlfay anthay IAY illway. Itay isay outay ofay ethay idolatrousay otingsday ofay ethay olday Egyptiansay uponay oiledbray ibisay anday oastedray iverray orsehay, atthay ouyay eesay ethay ummiesmay ofay osethay eaturescray inay eirthay ugehay ake-housesbay ethay amidspyray. 

Onay, enwhay IAY ogay otay easay, IAY ogay asay aay implesay ailorsay, ightray eforebay ethay astmay, umbplay ownday intoay ethay orecastlefay, aloftay erethay otay ethay oyalray ast-headmay. Uetray, eythay atherray orderay emay aboutay omesay, anday akemay emay umpjay omfray arspay otay arspay, ikelay aay asshoppergray inay aay Aymay eadowmay. Anday atay irstfay, isthay ortsay ofay ingthay isay unpleasantay enoughay. Itay ouchestay one’say ensesay ofay onorhay, articularlypay ifay ouyay omecay ofay anay olday establisheday amilyfay inay ethay andlay, ethay Anvay Ensselaersray, oray Andolphsray, oray Ardicanuteshay. Anday oremay anthay allay, ifay ustjay eviouspray otay uttingpay ouryay andhay intoay ethay ar-pottay, ouyay avehay eenbay ordinglay itay asay aay ountrycay oolmasterschay, akingmay ethay allesttay oysbay andstay inay aweay ofay ouyay. Ethay ansitiontray isay aay eenkay oneay, IAY assureay ouyay, omfray aay oolmasterschay otay aay ailorsay, anday equiresray aay ongstray ecoctionday ofay Enecasay anday ethay Oicsstay otay enableay ouyay otay ingray anday earbay itay. Utbay evenay isthay earsway offay inay imetay. 

Atwhay ofay itay, ifay omesay olday unkshay ofay aay ea-captainsay ordersay emay otay etgay aay oombray anday eepsway ownday ethay ecksday? Atwhay oesday atthay indignityay amountay otay, eighedway, IAY eanmay, inay ethay alesscay ofay ethay Ewnay Estamenttay? Oday ouyay inkthay ethay archangelay Abrielgay inksthay anythingay ethay esslay ofay emay, ecausebay IAY omptlypray anday espectfullyray obeyay atthay olday unkshay inay atthay articularpay instanceay? Owhay ain’tay aay aveslay? Elltay emay atthay. Ellway, enthay, oweverhay ethay olday ea-captainssay aymay orderay emay about—howeveray eythay aymay umpthay anday unchpay emay aboutay, IAY avehay ethay atisfactionsay ofay owingknay atthay itay isay allay ightray; atthay everybodyay elseay isay oneay ayway oray otheray ervedsay inay uchmay ethay amesay ay—eitherway inay aay icalphysay oray etaphysicalmay ointpay ofay iewvay, atthay isay; anday osay ethay universalay umpthay isay assedpay oundray, anday allay andshay ouldshay ubray eachay other’say oulder-bladesshay, anday ebay ontentcay. 

Againay, IAY alwaysay ogay otay easay asay aay ailorsay, ecausebay eythay akemay aay ointpay ofay ayingpay emay orfay myay oubletray, ereaswhay eythay evernay aypay assengerspay aay inglesay ennypay atthay IAY everay eardhay ofay. Onay ethay ontrarycay, assengerspay emselvesthay ustmay aypay. Anday erethay isay allay ethay ifferenceday inay ethay orldway etweenbay ayingpay anday eingbay aidpay. Ethay actay ofay ayingpay isay erhapspay ethay ostmay uncomfortableay inflictionay atthay ethay otway orcharday ievesthay entaileday uponay usay. Utbay eing_bay aid_,—whatpay illway omparecay ithway itay? Ethay urbaneay activityay ithway ichwhay aay anmay eceivesray oneymay isay eallyray arvellousmay, onsideringcay atthay eway osay earnestlyay elievebay oneymay otay ebay ethay ootray ofay allay earthlyay illsay, anday atthay onay onay accountay ancay aay oniedmay anmay enteray eavenhay. Ahay! owhay eerfullychay eway onsigncay ourselvesay otay erditionpay! 

Inallyfay, IAY alwaysay ogay otay easay asay aay ailorsay, ecausebay ofay ethay olesomewhay exerciseay anday urepay airay ofay ethay ore-castlefay eckday. Orfay asay inay isthay orldway, eadhay indsway areay arfay oremay evalentpray anthay indsway omfray asternay at(thay isay, ifay ouyay evernay iolatevay ethay Agoreanpythay aximmay), osay orfay ethay ostmay artpay ethay Ommodorecay onay ethay uarter-deckqay etsgay ishay atmosphereay atay econdsay andhay omfray ethay ailorssay onay ethay orecastlefay. Ehay inksthay ehay eathesbray itay irstfay; utbay otnay osay. Inay uchmay ethay amesay ayway oday ethay ommonaltycay eadlay eirthay eaderslay inay anymay otheray ingsthay, atay ethay amesay imetay atthay ethay eaderslay ittlelay uspectsay itay. Utbay ereforewhay itay asway atthay afteray avinghay epeatedlyray eltsmay ethay easay asay aay erchantmay ailorsay, IAY ouldshay ownay aketay itay intoay myay eadhay otay ogay onay aay alingwhay oyagevay; isthay ethay invisibleay olicepay officeray ofay ethay Atesfay, owhay ashay ethay onstantcay urveillancesay ofay emay, anday ecretlysay ogsday emay, anday influencesay emay inay omesay unaccountableay ay—heway ancay etterbay answeray anthay anyay oneay elseay. Anday, oubtlessday, myay oinggay onay isthay alingwhay oyagevay, ormedfay artpay ofay ethay andgray ogrammepray ofay Ovidencepray atthay asway awndray upay aay onglay imetay agoay. Itay amecay inay asay aay ortsay ofay iefbray interludeay anday olosay etweenbay oremay extensiveay erformancespay. Oughthay IAY annotcay elltay whyay itay asway exactlyay atthay osethay agestay anagersmay, ethay Atesfay, utpay emay ownday orfay isthay abbyshay artpay ofay aay alingwhay oyagevay, enwhay othersay ereway etsay ownday orfay agnificentmay artspay inay ighhay agediestray, anday ortshay anday easyay artspay inay enteelgay omediescay, anday ollyjay artspay inay arces—thoughfay IAY annotcay elltay whyay isthay asway exactlyay; etyay, ownay atthay IAY ecallray allay ethay ircumstancescay, IAY inkthay IAY ancay eesay aay ittlelay intoay ethay ingsspray anday otivesmay ichwhay eingbay unninglycay esentedpray otay emay underay ariousvay isguisesday, induceday emay otay etsay aboutay erformingpay ethay artpay IAY idday, esidesbay ajolingcay emay intoay ethay elusionday atthay itay asway aay oicechay esultingray omfray myay ownay unbiaseday eewillfray anday iscriminatingday udgmentjay.
